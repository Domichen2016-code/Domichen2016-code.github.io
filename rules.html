This is the Rules to each game

Tic-Tac-Toe

2 players take turns marking a 3x3 grid with X and O respectively. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a draw.

Guess the Number

The computer randomly selects a number within a specified range (e.g., 1 to 100). After each guess, the computer provides feedback indicating whether the guess is too high, too low, or correct. The game continues until the player guesses the correct number.

Ghost Catcher

In this game, you have to try and click the ghost as many times as possible before the time runs out.

Battleship

In this game, AI automatically generates ships for you to sink. You must guess the locations of the ships on a grid and sink them before your chances run out.

Rock-Paper-Scissors

Two players choose one of three options with their keyboard: rock, paper, or scissors. For player 1, the keys are Q for rock, W for paper, and E for scissors. For player 2, the keys are I for rock, O for paper, and P for scissors. The winner is determined by the following rules: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. If both players choose the same option, the game is a tie.
